I am building an AI-powered website that can be used as a digital running coach by analyzing videos of a customer's running form.Over 50% of new runners get injured every year, often due to poor running mechanics, yet most have no affordable way to get professional feedback on their form. Smartwatches can track pace and distance, but they can't tell runners whether their form is causing injury risk.We're bringing gait analysis lab capabilities to everyday runners at a fraction of the cost.  The website simply requires a customer to film themselves running and upload it onto the website. It works by using computer vision to track their skeletal structure frame by frame, flagging the timestamps where their posture weakens or their form breaks. This helps runners improve their technique, reduce injury risk, and run more efficiently.
